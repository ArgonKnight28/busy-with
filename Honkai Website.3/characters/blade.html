Blade Summary

A member of the Stellaron Hunters, whose real name is unknown. Blade wields an ancient sword that has filled with cracks, just like his body and his mind. He is a Wind Element AOE DPS who sacrifices HP to do heavy damage. Newer players might feel uncomfortable with the rapid fluctuations in his HP but can be easily be overcome with the use of good defensive supports.

Limited 5*   Element: Wind   Path: The Destruction

Skills:

Basic ATK - Shard Sword

(Lv6) Deals 100% of Blade's ATK as Wind DMG to a target enemy
Simple Basic Attack that can be enhanced with his Skill - Single Target (Unenhanced)

Skill - Hellscape

(Lv1) Consumes 30% of Blade's Max HP to immediately Advance action forward by 100%. In addition, DMG increases by 12% and Basic ATK "Shard Sword" is enhanced to "Forest of Swords" for 3 turn(s). Skill cannot be used while "Forest of Swords" is active. If HP is insufficient, after using skill, Blade's HP will be reduced to 1. This Skill cannot regenerate Energy.
The Enhanced Basic ATK deals damage to adjacent targets

Ultimate - Death Sentence

(Lv1) Deals 28.8% of Blade's ATK plus 72% of Max HP as Wind DMG to an enemy and adjacent targets and 14.4% of Blade's ATK plus 36% of Max HP as Wind DMG to adjacent targets. Additionally sets Blade's current HP to 50% of his Max HP.
Blade's Ultimate does massive AOE damage and it directly scales off Max HP (and ATK). But consistently playing him at low HP can be challenging for some players.

Talent - Shuhu's Gift

(Lv1) Every time he takes DMG, Blade gains 1 Charge stack for 3 turn(s), stacking up to 5 times. When the maximum Charge stacks is reached, consume all Charges and immediately unleash a follow-up attack on all enemies, dealing 21% of Blade's ATK plus 54% of the HP loss while stacking Charges as Wind DMG. Then, restores Blade's HP by 30% of his Max HP.
Devastating Counter-style attack that scales off lost HP and heals him for a percentage. Provides him with some sustain but requires him to survive till he hits max charges.

Technique - Karma Wind

(Lv1) Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering battle, consumes 20% of Blade's Max HP and deals 40% of Blade's Max HP as Wind DMG to all enemies.
Great to speed up his accumulation of charges and late game players will find that they can one shot monster waves with this once they are high enough level to speed up adventuring.

Blade Traces

Major Traces:

Endless Lifespan(Bonus Ability) - If Blade's HP is 50% or lower, incoming Healing is increased by an additional 20%


Dead and Back Again(Bonus Ability) - When Blade hits an enemy with Weakness Break using "Forest of Swords", he restores HP by 10% of Max HP plus 80.


Cyclone of Destruction(Bonus Ability) - Wind DMG dealt by Talent's follow-up attack is increased by 15%.


Minor Traces:

Effect RES Boost(Stat Bonus) - Effect Resist increased by 2.6/4.0%

HP Boost(Stat Bonus) - Max HP increased by 4.0/6.0/8.0%

ATK Boost(Stat Bonus) - ATK increased by 4.0/6.0/8.0%

DMG Boost: Wind(Stat Bonus) - Wind DMG increased by 3.2/4.0%


Blade Eidolons (E1 to E6)

E1 - Rejected by Death, Infected with Life

During the battle, every time Blade's HP goes lower than 50%, Max HP is increased by 20%. Stacks up to 2 time(s).


E2 - Missed Dreams and Countless Regrets

While "Forest of Swords" is active, if Blade's HP is restored, his next attack's DMG increases by 15% This effect stacks up to 3 time(s).


E3 - Smelting Steel, and Milling Grains

Ultimate +3 up to a maximum of Lv15. Basic ATK Lv +1 up to a maximum of Lv 10.


E4 - Ultimate Sword, Hidden Hell

When using Ultimate, if Blade's HP is above 50%, deals 100% of the HP loss caused by Ultimate to the enemy and adjacent targets as additional Wind DMG.


E5 - Death Sentence, Facing Karma

Skill +3 up to a maximum of Lv15. Basic ATK Lv +1 up to a maximum of Lv 10.


E6 - Body and Soul

When struck by a killing blow, instead of becoming incapacitated, Blade's HP is reduced to 1 and his Talent is immediately triggered. This effect can only trigger 1 time every 3 turn(s).
